\section{Conclusion}

My research advances a principled, mathematically rigorous approach to AI for
Science---a full-stack methodology spanning foundational theory, novel neural
architectures, and production-quality software. The unifying insight driving
my trajectory is that physical structure (asymptotic limits, conservation
laws, and variational principles) must be encoded as hard constraints rather
than optional soft regularization. This philosophy ensures methods that are not
merely empirically successful but provably robust in the stiff and high-dimensional
regimes where classical discretization breaks down.

Moving forward, I intend to solidify this line of inquiry into the
``Agent of Real Physical Science,'' anchoring domain-specific, physics-aware
solvers through open-source innovation, rigorous analysis, and cross-disciplinary
partnerships. I look forward to advancing this ambitious program at Shanghai Jiao
Tong University, cultivating deep international collaborations, and training the
next generation of computational mathematicians uniquely equipped to bridge
rigorous analysis and frontier artificial intelligence.
